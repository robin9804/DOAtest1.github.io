Digital Holography

Advanced holographic imaging techniques for next-generation display systems and optical processing applications. We develop innovative algorithms for real-time hologram generation and reconstruction.

Key Areas:

  • Real-time hologram generation algorithms
  • Computer-generated holography
  • Holographic optical elements
  • Phase retrieval techniques
  • Digital holographic microscopy

Augmented Reality

Cutting-edge AR technologies that seamlessly blend digital content with the real world for immersive experiences. Our research focuses on optical see-through displays and spatial tracking systems.

Key Areas:

  • Optical see-through displays
  • Spatial tracking and registration
  • AR visualization algorithms
  • Mixed reality interfaces
  • Real-time rendering optimization
ğŸ“º

3D Display Technology

Revolutionary 3D display technologies that provide natural depth perception without special glasses or headsets. We explore light field displays and volumetric imaging systems.

Key Areas:

  • Autostereoscopic displays
  • Light field display systems
  • Volumetric imaging
  • Integral imaging
  • Multi-view display optimization
